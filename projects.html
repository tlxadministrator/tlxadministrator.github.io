Indie Atl is a collaborative Music program that is featured on Xfinity Cable/VOD and on various social media outlets. International and National recording artists visit the studios in Atlanta, housed at Georgia State University and record a live show. The project has grown by leaps and bounds since it's early days.

Painstats.com was envisioned and created entirely in house at TLX Interactive. We wanted to create a free to use site that would give something back to the community. The site is intended to serve those who suffer chronic pain. Each user on the site logs in, records information about their pain, and can then see charts that are generated in real time about their pain data. Individual users within the community can share their pain information and communicate with one another about their experiences. Leveraging TLX expertise in the powerful capabilities of Drupal and Flash, we created a effective and very useful web site that integrates powerful back end database functionality and an intuitive front end graphical user interface.

Painstats.com is now in use by people all over the world. We consider this project very important to our overall mission of leveraging technology to make people's lives better whether those people are our clients, or our neighbors in the global community. Founding partner Todd Simpson spearheaded the design and implementation of the The Center for International Media Education (CIME) web presence. The Center for International Media Education (CIME) at Georgia State University cooperates with partner institutions in the United States and overseas to assist with strengthening journalism, journalism education, and the efforts of nongovernmental organizations.

Because this client is in the communication field, it was important to have an effective web presence that effectively and logically conveys a large amount of information in a manner that is easy to navigate and understand. CIME was delivered a solid quality product on time and within budget which met all customer requirements. For this static web site, requirements included an animated flash banner, detailed design work, image slide show, and streaming video, just to name a few. The result is aesthetically pleasing yet very functional web site that the client has been very happy with.
